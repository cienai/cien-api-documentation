## Description

### Purpose of Analysis

State the Coaching Intelligence goal (e.g., behavior/skill signal, training impact) and the relevant entities or events.

---

## Query

### Query Intent

Clarify which behaviors/skills are being proxied, the aggregation window, and the outcome metrics.

```sql
-- Replace this placeholder with the final SQL
SELECT
	rep,
	behavior_signal,
	AVG(score) AS avg_score
FROM ci_source
GROUP BY rep, behavior_signal
ORDER BY avg_score DESC;
```

### Sample Output

| rep | behavior_signal | avg_score |
| --- | --- | ---:|
| Alice | DiscoveryDepth | 0.82 |
| Bob | NextStepClarity | 0.67 |

## How to Interpret the Results

- What high vs low scores suggest.
- Suggested coaching interventions.
- Notes on data quality or seasonality.
